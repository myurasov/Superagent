# Superagent add-contact skill

## 1. Collect the contact's data

Use `AskQuestion` for the role-shape questions, free text for the rest.

**Name** (canonical form — first + last). **Aliases** if the person goes by other names.

**Role** (single-select):
- family
- friend
- professional (lawyer, accountant, financial advisor)
- provider (doctor, dentist, vet, mechanic, plumber, electrician, hairdresser, trainer, tutor, …)
- vendor (a business — not a specific person)
- neighbour
- colleague
- other

If **provider** or **family**, follow-up with **relationship**:
- provider: doctor | dentist | vet | mechanic | plumber | electrician | hairdresser | trainer | tutor | etc.
- family: spouse | parent | child | sibling | in-law | grandparent | etc.

**Email**, **Phone**, **Organization** (employer / practice / company), **Address** (only when relevant for appointments).

**Related domains** (multi-select; a vet maps to `pets`; a mechanic maps to `vehicles`; a dentist maps to `health`; a financial advisor maps to `finance`; etc.).

**Related assets** (optional; e.g. a mechanic for one specific vehicle).

**Notes** — preferences, history, gift ideas, allergies, kids' names — anything you want to remember.

**Last contacted** — ISO 8601 date if known.

**Contact cadence** (optional) — for friends / family you want to be reminded to reach out to: monthly | quarterly | annually | ad-hoc | none. Drives the Concierge "reconnect" nudges.

Auto-derive **id** = `<role-or-relationship>-<lastname>-<firstname>` (e.g. `dentist-smith-jane`, `friend-park-alex`). Confirm if non-obvious.

## 2. Check for duplicates

Search `_memory/contacts.yaml.contacts[]`:
- By name (case-insensitive substring).
- By email (exact).
- By phone (last 7 digits).

If a likely duplicate is found, ask: **same person / different person / merge**.

On **merge**: keep the existing row; append new info to `aliases`, `notes`, `related_domains`, etc. Log the merge.

## 3. Append the contact row

To `_memory/contacts.yaml.contacts[]`:

```yaml
- id: "<id>"
  name: "<Name>"
  aliases: <list>
  email: "<email>"
  phone: "<phone>"
  role: "<role>"
  relationship: "<relationship>"
  organization: "<org>"
  address: "<address>"
  related_domains: <list>
  related_assets: <list>
  notes: "<notes>"
  last_contacted: <date or null>
  contact_cadence: "<cadence or empty>"
  tags: []
```

## 4. Sync domain rolodexes

For each domain in `related_domains`:

1. Open `workspace/Domains/<Domain>/rolodex.md`.
2. Add a row to the appropriate table:
   - **Providers & Professionals** for providers and professionals.
   - **Family & Personal** for family / friends / neighbours.
   - **Vendors & Services** for vendors.
   - **Other** for anything else.
3. Update `_Last updated:_`.
