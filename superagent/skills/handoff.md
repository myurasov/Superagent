# Superagent handoff skill

This skill generates a sensitive document. Surface a banner at the start of the run:

> **Sensitive output ahead.** This packet aggregates account references, document locations, beneficiaries, and household instructions. The output goes to `workspace/Outbox/handoff/` (gitignored). Store the rendered file somewhere the trusted person can find it (encrypted USB, safe deposit box, password-manager note, attorney's office). Do NOT email or message it casually.

Ask: "Continue? (yes / no)"

## 1. Aggregate the data

Read across:

- `_memory/config.yaml.profile` — name, contact info, household composition.
- `_memory/contacts.yaml` — every contact with `relationship in [spouse, parent, child, sibling, in-law]` (for emergency contacts) AND every professional in `role in [professional, provider]` (lawyer, accountant, financial advisor, primary care, dentist).
- `_memory/accounts-index.yaml` — every active account, with `vault_ref` (where the credential lives) but NOT the credential itself.
- `_memory/documents-index.yaml` — every document with `physical_location` populated.
- `_memory/bills.yaml` — recurring bills with auto_pay status (so executor knows what will keep flowing vs needing attention).
- `_memory/subscriptions.yaml` — recurring subscriptions (similar reason).
- `_memory/health-records.yaml` — active conditions, allergies, current medications (the medic-prep brief minus the personal-history detail).
- `_memory/assets-index.yaml` — vehicles, valuable assets with location, insurance.
- `Domains/Family/info.md` — household routines from § Routines.
- `Domains/Pets/<pet>/info.md` — for each pet: vet, food, meds, "if I'm not home for 24 hours, do this".

## 3. Sensitive-data check

Before saving, scan the rendered output for:
- Full account numbers (regex: 16 consecutive digits, 9 digits, 11 digits) — replace with last-4 if found.
- Full SSN (regex: NNN-NN-NNNN) — replace with `XXX-XX-NNNN`.
- Full passport numbers — replace with last-4.
- Plaintext passwords — REFUSE to save and surface the offending lines for user repair.
