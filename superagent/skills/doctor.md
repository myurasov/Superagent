# Superagent doctor skill

Six diagnostic passes. After each pass, ask the user `apply none / apply some / apply all`.

## 1. Stale domains

Find domains where:
- `_memory/domains-index.yaml.<domain>.status: active`
- AND no `history.md` entry in last 12 months
- AND zero open tasks for this domain in `todo.yaml`.

Surface table; offer `archive` (move folder to `Archive/<YYYY-MM>/Domains/<slug>/` AND set `domains-index.yaml.<domain>.status: archived`) per row.

## 2. Stale assets

Find assets where:
- `assets-index.yaml.<asset>.status: active`
- AND `last_updated` > 12 months ago
- AND no `history.md` reference in last 12 months.

Surface; offer `mark disposed` (asks for date / reason / proceeds) or `keep`.

## 3. Stale Resources/

Find files in `Domains/<domain>/Resources/` and `Projects/<slug>/Resources/` where mtime > 24 months ago AND no reference in any index.

Surface; offer `archive` (move to `Archive/<YYYY-MM>/_doctor-resources/`) or `keep`.

**Note**: `doctor` NEVER touches `Sources/documents/` or `Sources/references/` (immutable per `contracts/sources.md` § 15.2). The strict pass for stale Sources is the user's job, surfaced via `monthly-review` § 7c as a candidate list, never auto-actioned.

## 4. Duplicates

For each of contacts, bills, subscriptions, appointments:

- Cluster by likely-same heuristics:
  - Contacts: same email OR same phone last-7 OR very similar names (Levenshtein < 3 on lower-cased name).
  - Bills: same payee + amount within ±5%.
  - Subscriptions: same provider.
  - Appointments: same provider + same date.
- Surface clusters; offer `merge into <id>` (canonical winner) or `keep separate` (with note).

## 5. Near-duplicate todos

Find tasks where `title` similarity > 80% AND status both open. Surface; offer `merge` (combine descriptions, take earlier created date, take stricter due date).

## 6. Simplification candidates

Find:
- Template-only files (a file in `Domains/<X>/info.md` whose body is identical to the template after substitution).
- Notes / file fragments that are < 100 chars.
- `Inbox/` files older than 14 days.
- `Tmp/` files older than 30 days.
- Empty subfolders.

Surface; offer `move to Archive/<YYYY-MM>/_doctor-deletions/` per row (reversible).

## 7. Broken cross-references

Validate:
- Every `bills.yaml.<bill>.account` exists in `accounts-index.yaml`.
- Every `bills.yaml.<bill>.pay_from_account` exists.
- Every `subscriptions.yaml.<sub>.pay_from_account` exists.
- Every `appointments.yaml.<appt>.provider` exists in `contacts.yaml`.
- Every `appointments.yaml.<appt>.asset` exists in `assets-index.yaml`.
- Every `assets-index.yaml.<asset>.domain` exists in `domains-index.yaml`.
- Every `documents-index.yaml.<doc>.path` exists on disk.

Surface broken references; offer `null out the field` or `repair` (interactive).

## 8. Expired documents

Find `documents-index.yaml` rows with `expires_at < today` AND `status: active`. Surface; offer `mark expired` (set `status: expired`) or `mark replaced` (asks for the new doc id).

## 9. Action signal drains

Find `_memory/action-signals.yaml` rows with `status: captured` older than 60 days. Surface; offer `process now` (delegates to `supertailor-review` for `target: tailor`, or to ad-hoc workspace action for `target: superagent`) or `decline` (with a reason).

## 10. Personal-signal review

Find `personal-signals.yaml` rows with `status: open` AND `surfaced_count > 5` AND `last_surfaced` > 90 days ago — these have been surfaced repeatedly without action. Offer `mark practicing` / `mark deferred` / `mark discarded`.
