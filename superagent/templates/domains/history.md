## How this file stays current

- Skills append a new H4 entry whenever they process a touchpoint for this domain.
- Manual entries are welcome — drop them at the top of the Log section in the same `#### YYYY-MM-DD — <title>` format.
- Do not delete entries (history is append-only). Mark superseded findings with `(superseded YYYY-MM-DD: see <newer-entry>)` instead.
